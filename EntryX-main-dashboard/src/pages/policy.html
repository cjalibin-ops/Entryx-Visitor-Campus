EntryX Policies

Essential rules and guidelines to ensure a safe, secure, and productive environment for everyone at the University of Northern Philippines.

Campus Access Guidelines

Official Registry: Access-01

RFID Registration

All visitors and new users must complete registration at the EntryX reception before entering the campus. After successful registration, an RFID card will be issued for secure and efficient access.

RFID Usage

The issued RFID card must be used for all future campus entries. Users are required to tap their RFID card at designated scanners when entering and exiting the premises.

Card Responsibility

RFID cards are non-transferable and must be kept secure at all times. Lost or damaged cards must be reported immediately to the administration office for replacement.

Parking Regulations

Traffic Division: PK-04

Authorized Parking

Parking is only allowed in designated areas assigned for visitors, students, and staff. Always follow campus signage and security instructions.

Code of Conduct

Institutional Ethics: CD-09

Respect and Discipline

All individuals must observe proper behavior, respect university personnel, and maintain a professional environment at all times.

Prohibited Actions

Any form of misconduct, including unauthorized access, misuse of RFID cards, or disruptive behavior, will result in penalties or restricted access.

Emergency & Safety

Health Center: SF-12

Emergency Response

In case of emergency, immediately notify campus security or proceed to the nearest help station for assistance.

Security Monitoring

The EntryX system continuously monitors campus entry and exit logs through RFID technology to ensure safety and accountability.

By entering the campus using the EntryX system, you agree to follow all university policies and security protocols. Violations may result in suspension of access privileges.

UNP Visitors RFID Entry System helps monitor campus visitors, track entry and exit activity, and improve campus security.
